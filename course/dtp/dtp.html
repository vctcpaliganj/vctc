DeskTop Publishing

It is a package of different application software that helps to perform the publishing work in computer (pc);
Publishing is the art of creating and printing different types of document

Component of DTP

There are manly Three component of DTP

1. Adobe Pagemaker
2. Adobe Photoshop
3. CorelDraw

History of DTP

1. Desktop publishing was developed in 1970 by xerox company.
2. The first package of DTP was launched for "Macintosh" (Mac) operating system (OS)
3. In 1984 The DTP package was developed of (IOS) operating system for (Apple device)
4. In 1990 the DTP package was developed of windows operating system.
5. "Paul Brainerd" is the founder of DTP who was owner of Aldus company.

Use of DTP

1. To create different types of card such as identity card, Birthday Card, invitation card etc.
2. To create Newpaper and Magazine paper.
3. To create Banner,Poster,Handwill, etc.
4. To create any types of document with custom size.

DTP application perform any task with a spcial concept as (WYSIWYG).

WYSIWYG:-
(What You See In What You Get on print)
