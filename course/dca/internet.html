The internet is a global network of interconnected devices and servers that allow people to communicate and share information across the world.

How to connect to the internet on a PC or laptop.

Choose an internet service provider (ISP) that provides services in your area and sign up for an internet plan.
Example: Jio,Airtel,BSNL,VI etc.

Connect your device to the internet using one of the following methods:

a. Ethernet cable:
Connect one end of the Ethernet cable to your computer's Ethernet port and the other end to your router's Ethernet port.

b. Wi-Fi:
Make sure your computer has a Wi-Fi adapter and turn on Wi-Fi. Select the name of your Wi-Fi network and enter the password, if prompted.

c. Mobile hotspot:
Turn on your mobile hotspot and connect your computer to the hotspot using Wi-Fi.
